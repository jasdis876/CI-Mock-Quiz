Executive Summary

This practice platform supports Contact Investigators / Social Disease Intervention Specialists preparing for competency assessment in STI/HIV case management, partner services, surveillance, confidentiality, linkage, re-engagement and field practice. It is designed for realistic exam preparation while reinforcing safe, current, Jamaica-specific public health action.

Source Authority Rule

Highest authority: 2026 STI Management Handbook and 2025 Contact Investigation Field Guide. Where older module content differs from current treatment or management guidance, the current MOHW STI Handbook and Field Guide take precedence.
